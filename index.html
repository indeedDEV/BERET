FEATURES

BERET is a Discord Bot a vast variety of easy-to-use utility, fun, moderation and music commands

UTILITY

b.help
b.ping
b.info @<user> or <id>
b.server

MODERATION

b.kick @<user>
b.ban @<user>
b.purge <number>

FUN

b.lotto

b.topic

MUSIC

b.join
b.leave
b.play <url> or <search>
